Unpaid Labor is a multidisciplinary art and design studio established in 2025 by Linda Dounia Rebeiz and Delali Vorgbe. We currently make AI that thinks otherwise by exposing it to frameworks that organize meaning differently.

We build things you can encounter online and in physical space.

Our practice begins from a provocation: that AI's intellectual and moral foundations are narrower than they need to be, and that indigenous cosmologies offer frameworks for more relational, ecological, and pluralistic reasoning.
